Instructions

Instructions on using pain free tattoo cream These creams have been used by thousands of people
around the world for the ultimate pain free tattoo experience. Numb ASF is safe and simple to use
- just follow these easy instructions and join the pain free tattoo revolution!

For Procedures Under 3 Hours

step 1

Prepare your skin

Wash and exfoliate the area to be tattooed thoroughly with soap, water and exfoliator to remove dirt and dead skin as this will help the cream penetrate. Dry area completely.

step 2

APPLY Numb ASF

Apply a thick amount of numbing cream (2mm thick) to the area and run in thoroughly. Cover with cling film. The heat under the plastic wrap helps activate the cream and keeps the cream from drying out.

step 3

RELAX... THEN Remove

Leave cream and wrap in place for 60-90 minutes before procedure. After this time, remove the cling film and wipe the cream away with a paper towel. Now you are ready for your pain free tattoo!

LONGER Procedures 3+ Hours

APPLy for LARGE TATTOOS:

It is recommended to split the tattoo into 3 hour sessions. Apply and remove the cream to each section prior to starting.

EXAMPLE LONGER SESSION:

9 hour day split into 3x3 hour sessions and 3 tubes of cream. Skin typically remains numb for 3-4 hours once cream is removed depending on your skin type and location on the body.

*Only leave on broken skin for 20 minutes at a time.
